About Us

GreenChar is dedicated to advancing the use of biochar in Indiana and Ghana. We provide consultancy services and advocacy to support sustainable agriculture, soil improvement, and environmental remediation. Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers and stakeholders with cost-effective biochar solutions, drawing on cutting-edge research and local expertise.

Our Services

Biochar Production Consulting

Expert guidance on optimizing biochar production using portable kilns, tailored for smallholder farmers in Ghana and agricultural systems in Indiana.

Soil & Water Solutions

Applying biochar for soil fertility enhancement in Indiana and agro-wastewater remediation in West Lafayette, ensuring sustainable practices.

Workshops & Advocacy

Educating farmers and stakeholders on biochar benefits, scalability, and environmental impact through workshops and community engagement.

Our Regions

Indiana, USA

Supporting sustainable agriculture through biochar soil amendments and wastewater treatment, addressing local challenges in the Midwest.

Ghana

Empowering smallholder farmers with cost-effective biochar production to enhance soil health, carbon sequestration, and climate resilience.
